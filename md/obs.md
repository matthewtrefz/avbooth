# OBS

## Multiview
Multiview shows all cameras in a single screen. The multiview should automatically be visible on startup, if not open Multiview.

The active camera will display a red border.
Make a camera active with a left click of the mouse on the multiview.

### Open Multiview
1. Ensure OBS is running
2. From the menu selct View -> Multiview(Fullscreen) -> R240HY2

## PTZ Control Dock
The position of each camera can be controlled within the main OBS screen using the PTZ Controls dock. The dock provides focus, pan, tilt, and zoom controls. A short click on a control button results in small movement. A longer press results in extended movement.

The control dock has three sections:
1. Camera List
2. Position Preset
3. Manual Position

### Manual Position
This section allows manual control for positioning a camera. The directional pad controls pan and tilt. The zoom controls sit on the right of the directional pad.

Below the direction pad are the focus controls

### Camera List
Select a camera with a single click on the camera name.
Once a camera is selected, its position can be controlled with the Position presets or the manual control.

### Position Presets
The preset list is almost identical for each of the cameras. The most-used presets are at the top of the list. Double-click a preset to move the camera.

- altar (back, front, wide)
- baptismal font
- pulpit
- choir
- soloist
- piano
- praiseband (front, wide, lead)
- communion
- prayer station
- congregation
