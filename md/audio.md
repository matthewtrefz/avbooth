# Audio

## Power On
- Remove the cover from the soundboard.
- Switch the power toggle on ![Drawing of furman power conditioner with power toggle highlighted](../assets/system-power-toggle.png)

## Select Scene
- Press the **VIEW** button in the **SCENCES** section of the sound board. ![Drawing of X32 Scenes section with View button hightlighted](../assets/x32-scene-view.png)
- Select scence 07 named **Service** ![Drawing of X32 main display with GO button hightlighted](../assets/x32-scene-go.png)
- Press the button label **GO**
- Press the button labeled **Confirm**![Drawing of X32 main display with View button hightlighted](../assets/x32-scene-confirm.png)
- Bring the slider marked MAIN to zero ![Drawing of X32 Scenes section with View button hightlighted](../assets/x32-main.png)

## Mute Groups

The mute groups are at the bottom right corner of the X32 Console. Each button is labeled. The top 4 buttons control microphones and instruments. 
The bottom 2 control the speakers for the Gathering Space and Choir Loft.
![Drawing of X32 mute groups section](../assets/x32-mute-group.png)

# Power Off

- Press the **VIEW** button in the **SCENCES** section of the sound board.![Drawing of X32 Scenes section with View button hightlighted](../assets/x32-scene-view.png)
- Select scence 07 named **Service** 
- Press the button label **GO**![Drawing of X32 main display with GO button hightlighted](../assets/x32-scene-go.png)
- Press the button labeled **Confirm**![Drawing of X32 main display with View button hightlighted](../assets/x32-scene-confirm.png)
- Switch the power toggle off ![Drawing of furman power conditioner with power toggle highlighted](../assets/system-power-toggle.png)
- Place the cover over the soundboard
