# Shutdown
- Return handheld mics to the charger **Orange** to choir loft, **Purple** to mic stand by baptismal font, the other mics are used on the praise band stage
- Return pastor's mic pack to the charger
- Power off the cameras on [stream deck][6]. Cameras will point to the wall.
- Close [OBS][5]
- Shut down broadcast computer [broadcast computer][4]
- Close [Presenter][3]
- Power off the [confidence monitor][10] (big tv above door)
- Power off the [projector][9]
- Shut down the [presentation computer][2]

- Press the **VIEW** button in the **SCENCES** section of the sound board.
- Select scence 07 named **Service**
- Press the button label **GO**
- Press the button labeled **Confirm**
- Power off the [audio system][1]
- Place the cover over the soundboard
