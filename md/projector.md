# Projector

The **Panasonic PT-RZ970LBU7** laser projector is mounted in the ceiling of the sanctuary and projects onto the **Screen Innovations 165" fixed-frame screen**.

## Power On

1. Point the Panasonic remote at the projector (or toward the screen — the signal reflects).
2. Press the **Power** button once.
3. The projector will display a green status light and warm up for approximately 30 seconds before showing an image.

> **Note:** If the image does not appear after 60 seconds, check that the Presentation PC is running and NDI Screen Capture is open.

## Power Off

1. Point the Panasonic remote at the projector.
2. Press the **Standby** button **twice** in succession.
3. The projector will go into standby mode (fan continues briefly while it cools down).

> **Do not unplug the projector** while the cooling fan is still running.
