# Audio On
- Remove the cover from the soundboard.
- Switch the power toggle to on
- Press the **VIEW** button in the **SCENCES** section of the sound board.
- Select scence 07 named **Service**
- Push the knob for Go
- Bring the slider marked MAIN to zero

# Mute Groups
The mute groups are at the bottome right corner of the X32 Console. Each button is labeled. The top 4 buttons control microphones and instruments. The bottom 2 control the speakers for the Gathering Space and Choir Loft.

# Audio Off
- Press the **VIEW** button in the **SCENCES** section of the sound board.
- Select scence 07 named **Service**
- Press the button label **GO**
- Press the button labeled **Confirm**
- Switch the power toggle off at the av booth
- Place the cover over the soundboard
