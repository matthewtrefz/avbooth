# Stream Deck

Using the stream deck you can switch cameras and toggle the text and slide overlays.

- Change to Page two on the stream deck by pushing the button to the right of the clock
- The overlays are toggled by pressing the button for text, slide and center.
- The fourth button in the top row will switch to a full screen of the slides.
- The second row of buttons for cameras 1-4 will change the selected camera when pushed.
