# Shutdown

## Mics
- Return pastor's mic pack to the charger
- Return handheld mics to the charger

## Broadcast
- Power off the cameras. Cameras will point to the wall.
- Close OBS
- Shut down broadcast computer

## Presentation
- Close Presenter
- Power off the confidence monitor (big tv above door)
- Power off the projector
- Shut down the presentaion computer

## Audio
- Press the **VIEW** button in the **SCENCES** section of the sound board.
- Select scence 07 named **Service**
- Press the button label **GO**
- Press the button labeled **Confirm**
- Switch the power toggle off at the av booth
- Place the cover over the soundboard
